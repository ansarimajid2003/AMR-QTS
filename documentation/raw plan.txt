# 2️⃣ SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

The system is divided into 4 independent layers.

---

## LAYER A — Risk Governance Layer (Highest Priority)

This overrides everything else.

### A1. Risk Per Trade

* Base risk: 0.5% equity
* Maximum risk: 1% (only after equity growth buffer)

### A2. Daily Loss Cap

* Hard stop at 3% equity
* Trading disabled for remainder of day

### A3. Max Open Exposure

* Max 3 simultaneous trades
* Total exposure ≤ 2% equity

### A4. Correlation Control

Avoid stacking correlated risk:

* No multiple USD directional exposure
* No simultaneous gold + USD pair trend stacking

### A5. Volatility Safety Switch

If ATR > 2× 30-day ATR average:

* Reduce position size by 50%
* Disable mean reversion module

This layer ensures survival.

---

## LAYER B — Market Regime Detection Engine

Purpose: Identify market condition objectively.

### Metrics Used

1. ADX (14)

   * ADX > 25 → Trending
   * ADX < 20 → Ranging

2. ATR Ratio

   * ATR current / ATR 30-day avg
   * > 1.5 → High Volatility

3. Structure Filter

   * HH/HL → Uptrend
   * LH/LL → Downtrend

### Output Classification

* TRENDING LOW VOL
* TRENDING HIGH VOL
* RANGING LOW VOL
* RANGING HIGH VOL

No subjective analysis.

---

## LAYER C — Strategy Modules

Each regime activates a specific strategy.

---

### MODULE 1 — Trend Continuation Model

Activated when:

* ADX > 25
* Clear structural trend

Entry:

* Break of 20-bar high/low
* Volume > 1.5× average
* Higher timeframe EMA alignment

Stop:

* ATR × 1.5

Exit:

* 1:2 RR
* OR ATR trailing

Risk:

* 0.5% base

---

### MODULE 2 — Mean Reversion Model

Activated when:

* ADX < 20
* No structural trend
* Not high volatility

Entry:

* RSI < 20 → Long
* RSI > 80 → Short
* Price at Bollinger extreme

Stop:

* ATR × 1.2

Exit:

* Mid Bollinger band
* 1:1.5 RR

Risk:

* 0.5% base

---

### MODULE 3 — High Volatility Protocol

Activated when:

* ATR ratio > 1.5

Rules:

* Mean reversion disabled
* Breakout only
* Risk reduced by 50%
* Wider ATR stop

---

## LAYER D — Equity Curve Control

Purpose: Prevent drawdown spirals.

If equity < 20-period moving average of equity curve:

* Reduce risk per trade by 50%

If equity makes new high:

* Restore base risk

This keeps drawdowns controlled during losing streaks.
